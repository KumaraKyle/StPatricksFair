St Patrick’s School Fair

Get ready for a day of fun, excitement, and community spirit!
The St. Patrick’s School Fair is a beloved tradition that brings together students, families, and friends for a celebration of all things wonderful. Whether you're a longtime supporter or new to our school, there’s something for everyone to enjoy.

Mark Your Calendars! Join us on 16th March for an unforgettable day at our fair. The event will be held at St. Patrick’s School Grounds, located at 57 Plynlimon Road, Bryndwr. Bring your family, friends, and neighbors for a day packed with fun activities, games, food, and entertainment.
